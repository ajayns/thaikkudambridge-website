Lets start with the name, shall we...
The band literally was born near Thaikkudam bridge. Thaikkudam is a place in Cochin, Kerala. All the hunky, funky, lean, mean, fat, brat members of the band got themselves jam packed in a room near there and kick started the journey! In spite of the diverse cultural backgrounds, the members hit it off really well and that's the real reason we do what we do!!
